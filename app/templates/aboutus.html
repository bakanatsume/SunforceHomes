About Us

CEO And Fouder Of SunforceHomes

The graduate physicist Mr Rudra Bahadur Pun has been committed to the development and expansion of photovoltaics for more than 37 years. As one of the pioneers in the solar industry, he formed an engineering office in 1982 from which SunforceHomes SOLAR emerged.

Rudra Bahadur Pun Magar is well connected in the industry: He founded the PV symposium in Bad Staffelstein at Banz Abbey in 1986. He represented the interests of the Nepelese PV industry as an Executive Board member and board spokesman of the Nepal Solar Industry Federation for six years from 1996. He supported the merger of the solar thermal energy and photovoltaic industry federations into "NSI - Nepal Solar Industry Federation".

Rudra Bahadur Pun has received multiple awards for his complete dedication - including the "Bavarian Environment Medal". He was awarded the "Entrepreneur of the Year" title from Ernst & Young in 2009 and in 2012 Rudra Pun received the Federal Cross of Merit in 2012 for his long-term and entrepreneurial commitment.
